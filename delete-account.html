Delete Your MyKalar Account

You can delete your MyKalar account and all associated data by following these steps:

Steps to delete your account:

  1. Open the MyKalar app on your phone
  2. Tap on Profile (bottom right)
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap "Deactivate Account"
  5. Confirm deactivation

What gets deleted:

  • Your profile and personal information
  • Your posts and comments
  • Your matrimony profile (if created)
  • Your chat messages
  • All other data associated with your account

Timeline:

Your account will be deactivated immediately. All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
