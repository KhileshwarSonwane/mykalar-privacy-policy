MyKalar Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

MyKalar collects the following information: Name, email address, mobile number, profile photos, and location (for ride pooling feature).

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide community networking features, matrimony matching, event management, business listings, ride pooling, and community chat services.

Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS. We use Supabase for secure data storage.

Third Party Services

We use Google AdMob for advertisements and Razorpay for payment processing.

Child Safety Standards

MyKalar is a community networking app for adults (18+) only. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Our Safety Measures:

  • App is restricted to users 18 and above
  • All user generated content is moderated
  • Users can report inappropriate content directly within the app
  • Reported content is reviewed by admins
  • Accounts violating policies are suspended

Reporting CSAE:

To report child safety concerns:
